The Best Movies According to Hlakaniphani

My Top Three Movies of all-time

Ubambo lwami Zulu Drama

This is my favourite Drama where Thoko is torn between two men: David, her childhood sweetheart, and Sandile, a charismatic stranger. As she grapples with her emotions and societal expectations, Thoko's choices lead to unforeseen consequences

Chackie Chan Rush hour

Two cops from different cultures, who cannot stand each other, team up to save the kidnapped 11-year-old daughter of a diplomat. With time running out, they must nab the criminals and save the girl.

Savage beauty

As you dive further into the series, Zinhle has deeper motivations on why she chose to become the face of Bhengu Beauty
